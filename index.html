03. About Me

Hi, I'm lukalot, I'm a developer and artist, and I often try to dream up neat works of art and software. It's debated whether I actually do or do not build neat things, but you can check them out and decide for yourself. Besides artistry—and among other interests—I love weird computer simulations, 1950-60's music, and science fiction. I use Linux. I'm inspired by some of my favorite games' art styles, and develop my own games sometimes. I'm recently worked on Lazy Galaxy 2, as a pixel artist, and I'm now working at castle.xyz, a new social media for interactive art and games. Some of my complete projects posted above, or, you can check out an extensive list over at my Wikipedia user page.
