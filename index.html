02. About Me

Hello, I'm Luke N. Arnold, and I go by Lukalot among the far corners of the internet. I'm a developer and artist, and I often try to dream up neat works of art and software. It's debatable whether I actually do or do not build neat things, but you can check them out and decide for yourself. Besides artistry—and among other interests—I love weird computer simulations, 1950-60's music, and science fiction. I use Linux. I'm inspired by some of my favorite games' art styles, and develop my own games sometimes. I'm currently working on a game from my favorite indie franchise: Lazy Galaxy 2, as a pixel artist.
